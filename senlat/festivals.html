cold and humid -> frost 		Pelnu diena February 7. 1st day of new year.

Lieldienas March 23 (22 on leap year) Spring eqinox, so from this point on, the days are longer than the nights.
lit. Long Days. Celebrations revolve around word "big: big days, well wishes for big harvests..., big swing is hung to represent Dievs cradle.

Lieldienas con't. March 24, 25 (23, 24, 25 on leap year). Extra days added to ancient calendar as celebration.
