Ancient Latvian Gods and Goddesses

The whole Dievs, Vecais Tevs (God, Grandfother)
Fate Laima (Goddess of Fate) appears as a part of Dievs
Laima can appear as Dekla, Karkla, Laidene
Life (default, devine motive to live) Saule (Sun - represents Life) appears as part of Dievs
Saule can appear as Saules Meitas, Zvaigzne (Star)
Factors influencing (help or retard) life Dievadeli (The sons of Dievs) personify qualities of Dievs
Celestial bodies
Meness, Auseklis, Pukis, Constellations/Light (Metenis, Usins, Janis, Mikelis, Martins, 4 Brothers Ziemassvetki)
Spiritual bodies
Vadatajs, Peteris, Jekabs
Earthly bodies
Perkons, Jumis
Planet earth and its atmosphere Mara, Mate (The Goddess Mara, Mother) appears as part of Dievs
